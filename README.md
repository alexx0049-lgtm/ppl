Static website files:
- index.html
- style.css

## What is included
- 10 expanded flight-training chapters based on the user's uploaded Pooley's Air Pilot's Manual Vol. 1.
- Romanian / bilingual hover / English language modes.
- Source badges:
  - BOOK VERIFIED
  - EASA SYLLABUS
  - OFFICIAL CROSS-CHECK
  - AFM / POH CHECK
- Chapter quizzes with explanations.
- Weak-area tracking stored locally in the browser.
- EASA PPL(A) training roadmap.
- 9-subject theoretical-knowledge map.
- Aviation English glossary.

## Research basis
- User-provided Pooley's Air Pilot's Manual Vol. 1 for the first 10 flight-training chapters.
- EASA Part-FCL / AMC1 FCL.210 PPL(A) for syllabus alignment and PPL(A) training structure.
- FAA Airplane Flying Handbook and Pilot's Handbook of Aeronautical Knowledge as a second official cross-check for general aerodynamics, basic manoeuvres, slow flight, and energy-management explanations.
- Current online PPL-learning patterns informed the UX: modular lessons, progress checks, visual explanations, and weak-area review. No third-party course content was copied.

## Important
This is a study companion, not an approved training course, checklist, AFM/POH, operational manual, or replacement for an instructor/DTO/ATO.
Aircraft-specific speeds, limitations, settings, and exact procedures must be checked in the approved aircraft documentation and with the instructor.
